Who We Are

Portcall is a modern contact management web application designed to help you organize and manage your contacts effortlessly.

Built using the LAMP stack (Linux, Apache, MySQL, PHP), our platform provides a reliable and efficient solution for storing, searching, editing, and deleting contact information from anywhere with an internet connection.

Key Features

  • Secure user authentication
  • Fast contact search
  • Easy-to-use interface
  • Add, edit, and delete contacts
